# Reading Group

We are an open reading group that meets every first Tuesday of the month at 15:00 c.t. at the [office](/contact) to read
a selected paper. Papers can be voted on up to 14 days before the reading group meeting, then the one with the most
votes gets selected.

!!! info "Trusted Research Environments and FAIR Sensitive Data"

    Topics should include, but are not limited to:

    * Trusted Research Environments
    * FAIR Principles
    * Data Management
    * Sensitive Data Infrastructures/Processes/etc.

!!! danger "Open Access"

    Only papers that are open access can be submitted to be voted on. Other papers are not accepted.
