Martin is a PreDoc researcher at the Information and Software Engineering Group (IFS) at the department of Information
Systems Engineering at the Vienna University of Technology (TU Wien). He received his MSc in *Software Engineering &
Internet Computing* from TU Wien in 2022 and BSc in *Software & Information Engineering* from TU Wien in 2019.

His research interests cover a broad spectrum of secure data infrastructures, virtual research environments, FAIR data
and digital repositories.

## Project Highlights

### [EOSC Life](https://www.eosc-life.eu/)

2021-2023: *Building a Digital Space for the Life Sciences*. The project will publish FAIR data and a catalogue of
services provided by participating RIs for the management, storage and reuse of data in the European Open Science
Cloud (EOSC).

Visit https://ossdip.at to view the progress on the open-source secure data infrastructure.

### [FDA](https://forschungsdaten.at/fda/)

2020-2022: *FAIR Data Austria*. The FAIR Data Austria project is designed to strengthen knowledge transfer between
universities, industry, and society und supports the sustainable implementation of the European Open Science Cloud (
EOSC). Within the project, implementation of the FAIR principles (which mandate that research data be Findable,
Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) plays a major role.

Visit https://dbrepo.ossdip.at to view the interactive prototype of our open-source implementation.
